Arrangement.Pianola

As per design specifications, the Piano arrangement now has a "compact mode", in which two support cards are shown regardless of the dimensions of the arrangement. Behold, the Pianola!

This helper class encapsulates this "compact mode" of the Piano arrangement, and is meant to be invoked by our Picard tester application. Under normal circumstances, it would be preferred to call the actual Piano arrangement and pass the compact-mode property as needed.
